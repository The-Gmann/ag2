The Fugitive

A single Fugitive is facing all the other players who are part of the Cops team. When a Cop kills the Fugitive, Fugitive turns to Cop and the killer becomes the new Fugitives with a full set of weapons/ammo/armor.

You spawn with all weapons (2 grenades in Regular, 1 grenade in Pro), full armor and the longjump module. Egon is not allowed in this game mod.

Points are as follow:
- Cop kills Fugitive : 1 point
- Cop kills an other Cop : -1 point
- The Fugitive kills Cop : number of Cops points
