Free For All

Kill every other players and scores the biggest amount of frags.

You spawn with the gravgun, crowbar, pistol, mp5 and 2 grenades in Regular (0 grenade in Pro). Pick up weapons and ammunitions that are located in the map to be stronger. Pick up health kits to cure yourself and armor kits to strengthen your protection suit.

Team Deathmatch

Kill every players who do not belong to your team.

You spawn with the gravgun,crowbar, pistol, mp5 and 2 grenades in Regular (0 grenade in Pro). Pick up weapons and ammunitions that are located in the map to be stronger. Pick up health kits to cure yourself and armor kits to strengthen your protection suit.

The winning team is the team that scores the biggest amount of frags.

Arcade

Kill every other players and scores the biggest amount of frags.

You spawn with all weapons (2 grenades in Regular, 1 grenade in Pro), full armor and the Longjump module. You can pick up ammunitions if required. Also, you can pick health kits to cure yourself and armor kits to strengthen your protection suit.

Arena

This is duel based mod in which two players are facing each other. The winner scores one point and plays against the next player in the waiting list. The looser is going to the end of the waiting list.

You spawn with all weapons (2 grenades in Regular, 1 grenade in Pro), full armor and the longjump module. You can't pick up additional ammunitions, health kits or armor.

Last Team Standing

This is a round based mod which means that you don't respawn when you are killed. The winning team is the one that has at least one player still alive in the game.

You spawn with all weapons (2 grenades in Regular, 1 grenade in Pro), full armor and the longjump module.

Last Man Standing

This is a round based mod which means that you don't respawn when you are killed. The winner is the last ALIVE player in the game.

You spawn with all weapons (2 grenades in Regular, 1 grenade in Pro), full armor (and the longjump module in Promod).

Capture The Flag

This is a 2 teams based mod : blue and red. Each team has one flag. The objective for each team is to get the flag from the other team and return it to its base without having its own flag captured. Therefore, you need to defend your flag and attack the other team base to get their flag.

If an ennemy gets your flag and you kill him, then the flag will be dropped at the place where the ennemy has been killed. You need to run to your flag to make it spawn at your base wherever you are in the map.

The team that captures and secures the ennemy flag in its base scores one point. The winning team is the team that scores the biggest ammount of points.

You spawn with the gravgun, crowbar, pistol, mp5 and 2 grenades in Regular (0 grenade in Pro). Pick up weapons and ammunitions that are located in the map to be stronger. Pick up health kits to cure yourself and armor kits to strengthen your protection suit.

Domination

This is a 2 teams based mod : blue and red. The objective for each team is to get the neutral flag which is located in the middle of the map and hold it as much as it can. The winning team is one that held the flag the longest amount of time.

You spawn with the gravgun, crowbar, pistol, mp5 and 2 grenades in Regular (0 grenade in Pro). Pick up weapons and ammunitions that are located in the map to be stronger. Pick up health kits to cure yourself and armor kits to strengthen your protection suit.

Warball

This is a 2 teams based mod : blue and red. The objective for each team is to score a goal by throwing the ball that is located in the middle of the map to the ennemy base.

You spawn with the gravgun, crowbar, pistol, mp5 and 2 grenades in Regular (0 grenade in Pro). Pick up weapons and ammunitions that are located in the map to be stronger. Pick up health kits to cure yourself and armor kits to strengthen your protection suit.

Football

This is a 2 teams based mod : blue and red. The objective for each team is to score a goal by throwing the ball that is located in the middle of the map to the ennemy base.

You spawn with the gravgun. You can pick up the ball from an ennemy by either kicking him with your crowbar or by pushing him when using the gravgun. The ball carrier cannot bunnyhop so you are encouraged to throw the ball to your teammates to go faster.

Instant Gib

Take care. This is a one shot - one kill mod. You score one point per frag.

You spawn with the gravgun, magnum, crossbow, gauss weapons and the longjump module. Pick up weapons and ammunitions that are located in the map to be stronger.

Tourney

This is duel based mod in which all players are attending a 1o1 tourney. The winner of the tourney wins a Gold Medal and the finalist a Silver Medal.

You spawn with all weapons (2 grenades in Regular, 1 grenade in Pro), full armor and the longjump module. You can't pick up additional ammunitions, health kits or armor.

The Fugitive

A single Rebel is facing all the other players who are part of the Combine's team. When a Combine kills the Rebel, Rebel turns to Combine and the killer turns to Rebel with a full set of weapons/ammo/armor.

You spawn with all weapons (2 grenades in Regular, 1 grenade in Pro), full armor and the longjump module.

Points are as follow:
- Combine kills Rebel : 1 point
- Combine kills Combine : -1 point
- Rebel kills Combine :
- 1 point if less than 2 Combine
- 2 points if 3 or 4 Combine
- 3 point if >= 5 Combine
